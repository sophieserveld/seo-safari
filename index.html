📖 SEO Definitions — read these first!
🔑 Keywords ✓
The exact phrases customers type into search engines or AI platforms. We research real volumes to build pages around them. 'Gatwick Parking' = 110,000/month!
🏅 Authority ✓
How much Google trusts your site — built through quality backlinks, strong content, and a consistent brand presence.
📊 Rankings ✓
Our position on a SERP (Search Engine Results Page) — paid ads, organic listings (us!), AI Overviews and maps. We fight to rank at the top, for free!
⭐ Reviews ✓
Customer reviews signal trust to Google. Positive reviews help pages rank higher and reassure customers they're in safe hands.
🔗 Backlinks ✓
Trusted sites linking to HX — a vote of confidence. Google bans paid links (risks SERPs bans!) so we earn them creatively: linkbait, competitor analysis, or expert guest content.
❓ FAQs ✓
Target longer queries, address purchase hurdles, and boost chances of appearing in Google's People Also Ask sections.
🖼️ Images ✓
Optimised images enhance UX and give search engines more context. Visual content is processed faster than text — critical for reducing bounce rates.
🧩 Entity Schema Markup ✓
Hidden code giving Google more context. Translates content into structured entities (people, places, products). Enables rich results like star ratings.
⚡ Page Speed ✓
Slow pages are abandoned by customers, sending negative signals to Google. Fast pages rank and convert better.
🎯 Optimisation ✓
The ongoing process of improving pages so they rank higher — better content, fixing technical issues, earning links, and the best experience for users.
